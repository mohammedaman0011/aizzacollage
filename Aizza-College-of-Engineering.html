Admission Process

Join our community of future engineers and innovators. Follow these steps to begin your journey with us.

Application Steps

1

Online Registration

Create an account on our admission portal and fill in your basic details.

2

Submit Application

Complete the application form and upload required documents.

3

Entrance Examination

Take our entrance examination to demonstrate your aptitude and knowledge.

4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview.

5

Admission Offer

Selected candidates will receive an admission offer with fee details.
