Welcome to Aizza College of Engineering

About Us

Aizza College of Engineering, located in Mulkalla, Mancherial, is committed to delivering quality technical education. We aim to foster innovation, research, and leadership in students to meet global challenges.

Admission Process

Admissions are open for Diploma, B.Tech, and MBA programs. Candidates must meet eligibility criteria and appear for the respective entrance exams (EAMCET/ICET/Polytechnic entrance). Applications can be submitted online or at the college campus.

Courses Offered

  • Diploma Programs: Civil, Mechanical, EEE
  • B.Tech Programs: CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, EEE
  • MBA Program: HR, Finance, Marketing, IT

Library

Our library houses over 15,000 volumes, national & international journals, e-books, and digital resources. It's a hub for academic learning and research.

Labs

The college features well-equipped laboratories for all departments, including computer labs with the latest software, and modern equipment for electronics, mechanical, and civil engineering labs.

Activities

Students participate in technical fests, workshops, sports, cultural events, and social outreach programs to ensure holistic development beyond academics.
